Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. When you use the FreeRes platform, we may collect and use some information. Here's a quick overview of our privacy policy:

1. Information We Collect

When you browse and download resources, we may collect your IP address, browsing history, resource download history, and other information to improve our services and resource recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: optimize resource recommendations, improve platform features, ensure service security, and track resource popularity to provide better free resources.

3. Information Sharing

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following cases: when required by law, to protect platform and user rights, or with trusted partners who help us provide services (and they won't use the information for other purposes).

4. Your Rights

You can contact us at any time to request access to, correct, or delete the personal information we've collected about you, or to opt out of our resource recommendation services.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your free resource browsing and downloading experience. By continuing to use our platform, you agree to our use of cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and provide more personalized resource recommendations.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: Ensure the platform runs properly, like remembering your resource filter preferences
  • Functional Cookies: Track your browsing history to provide personalized resource recommendations
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand resource popularity and optimize resource categories

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality, such as not being able to save resource filter conditions or receive personalized recommendations.
