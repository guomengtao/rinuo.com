Hi Baby! Meet Jupyter!

Let's learn what this cool tool does and how to use it!

What's Jupyter, anyway?

Jupyter is like a magic notebook for big kids and grown-ups who love to play with numbers and code!

It's where you can write, draw, and make cool things happen with just a few clicks!

Think of it as a super fun digital playground for your brain!

How Do We Play With Jupyter?

1

Open It Up

Click on the Jupyter icon like you're opening a new toy box!

2

Make New Stuff

Create a new notebook - it's like getting a fresh piece of paper!

3

Play & Learn!

Add words, numbers, and pictures - then watch the magic happen!

Cool Things Jupyter Can Do!

Write stories and notes

Do math puzzles

Show pretty pictures

Make things happen with code
