What is jQuery?

A super tool that helps make websites more fun and interactive!

So, What Exactly Is jQuery?

jQuery is like a magic wand for websites! It's a special tool that programmers use to make websites:

  • Move things around (like pictures that dance!)
  • Change colors when you click them
  • Show hidden surprises
  • Respond when you move your mouse

Think of jQuery as a helper that makes websites more fun and playful!

How Does jQuery Work?

1

First, you add jQuery to your website, like adding a new toy to your collection.

2
