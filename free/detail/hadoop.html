Teamwork!

What is Hadoop?

Hadoop is like a team of computer friends that work together to solve big problems!

Works Together

Instead of one computer doing all the work, many computers share the job - like a team playing a game!

Handles Lots of Stuff

Can manage tons of pictures, videos, and information - more than any single computer could handle alone!

Super Tough

If one computer takes a break, the others keep working - like when your team keeps playing if someone needs a rest!

How Does Hadoop Work?

It's like when you and your friends work together on a big project!

1 Split the Work

The Leader Node splits a big job into smaller pieces, like dividing a pizza into slices for friends.

2 Share the Work

Each Worker Node gets one small piece to work on, just like how you and friends might each color a part of a big picture.

3 Do the Work

All Worker Nodes work at the same time, getting their part done quickly - teamwork makes things faster!

4 Combine Results

The Leader Node puts all the pieces back together to finish the big job, like completing a puzzle with friends!
