What is Hadoop?

Hadoop is like a magical toy box for storing and playing with tons of information!

Imagine you have a huge collection of toys. You can't keep them all in one box, right? Hadoop is like having many boxes that work together to hold all your toys!

It helps computers store and use BIG data - that's lots and lots of information, like all the pictures, videos, and games on the internet!

With Hadoop, we can:

  • Store millions of pictures and videos
  • Find your favorite cartoon quickly
  • Play games with friends online
  • Learn new things from all over the world
hadoop-example.please
$ hadoop magic start

🎈 Stored 1,000,000 pictures
⚡ Found your favorite video in 2 seconds

Hadoop in Action

See how Hadoop works with these cool examples!

Photo Storage

Hadoop can store millions of photos, like all the pictures you take on your birthday!

hadoop store photos /my-birthday

→ Stored 5,000 photos!

Game Scores

It keeps track of high scores for your favorite games, even when millions play!

hadoop track scores /my-game

→ Updated 100,000 scores!

Cartoon Time

Hadoop helps you find and watch your favorite cartoons super fast!

hadoop find cartoons "funny animals"

→ Found 25 great cartoons!
