What's pnpm?

It's like a magic toy box for computer programs! üéÅ

pnpm helps computers share and play with toys (called "packages")!

So, What Does pnpm Do?

Super Fast!

pnpm is like a race car! It gets toys (packages) for your computer much faster than other helpers.

Saves Space

It's good at sharing! If two games need the same toy, pnpm only keeps one copy. Smart, right?

Keeps Things Safe

pnpm makes sure all the toys work well together. No broken games here!

Easy to Use

With simple commands, you can get new toys or clean up old ones in seconds!

Fun Commands to Try

pnpm install [toy-name]

Gets a new toy (package) for your project! Like asking for a new game.

pnpm run play

Starts your project! It's like pressing the "start" button on your game.

pnpm remove [toy-name]

Puts a toy back on the shelf. Great for cleaning up!

pnpm update

Gives all your toys a makeover! Updates them to their newest versions.
