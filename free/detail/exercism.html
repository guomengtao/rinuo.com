What the Heck is Exercism?

Let's break it down simply – Exercism is where coders go to get better, plain and simple.

The Name Says It All

Exercism comes from "exercise" – because that's what you're doing: exercising your coding muscles. It's not about cramming for interviews or mindless repetition – it's about building real, lasting skills through deliberate practice.

Where It All Started

Back in 2014, Jeremy Walker got tired of coding platforms that just told you "right" or "wrong" without any real guidance. He built Exercism to be different – a place where beginners and pros alike could get detailed feedback on their code from actual human mentors.

Jeremy Walker

Founder of Exercism

"We believe that everyone can code, and that great code comes from feedback, not just repetition. Exercism is built around that idea."

How This Thing Actually Works

It's simpler than you think – no fancy algorithms or confusing interfaces. Just a straightforward way to get better at coding.

1. Pick Your Language

Choose from 65+ programming languages – from Python and JavaScript to Rust and Go. No matter what you're trying to learn, they've got you covered.

2. Solve the Exercises

Work through realistic problems that actually matter. These aren't just toy challenges – they're based on real-world coding scenarios.

3. Get Feedback

Here's the magic – real developers review your code and give you detailed, personalized feedback. You'll learn not just what works, but why it works.

Why Exercism Stands Out

Let's be real – there are a million coding platforms out there. But Exercism does things differently, and better.

Actual Human Mentors

No robots here. Get feedback from experienced developers who know what they're talking about. It's like having a coding buddy who actually knows their stuff.

65+ Programming Languages

From the basics like Python and JavaScript to more niche languages like Elixir and Elm. Whatever you're trying to learn, they've got exercises for it.

CLI Tool for Power Users

For those who live in the terminal (we see you), Exercism has a badass CLI tool that lets you work without ever leaving your command line.

Structured Learning Paths

Exercises build on each other, taking you from basics to more advanced concepts without feeling overwhelming. It's like a roadmap for your coding journey.

Active Community

Join a community of over a million developers all learning and growing together. Ask questions, share insights, and find your people.

Focus on Best Practices

It's not just about getting code to work – it's about writing clean, maintainable code that other developers won't hate you for. That's the real win.

Let's Be Honest – It's Not Perfect

No tool is for everyone. Here's where Exercism might fall short for some folks.

Takes Time

Getting feedback from mentors isn't instant. If you're looking for immediate gratification, this might frustrate you.

Some Limits on Free

While you can learn a lot for free, the full mentor experience and some advanced tracks require a subscription.

Not for Absolute Beginners

You should know the absolute basics before diving in. It's more about improving than learning from square one.

How It Stacks Up
