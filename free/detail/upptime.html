Meet Upptime!

Your Website's Best Friend

Upptime is like a superhero that watches over websites and apps to make sure they're always working properly!

What Does Upptime Do?

It's like having a helper that checks if your favorite websites are awake and working!

Checks All the Time

Upptime looks at websites super often (like every minute!) to make sure they're working.

Keeps Records

It writes down when sites are working and when they take a little nap (go down).

Makes Cool Charts

Upptime creates colorful graphs that show how well a website is doing over time.

How Does It Work?

It's like teaching a robot to check your favorite websites!

It's All in the Code!

You tell Upptime which websites to check with a simple list:

{
  "sites": [
    {
      "name": "My Cool Website",
      "url": "https://mycoolwebsite.com"
    },
    {
      "name": "Fun Game Site",
      "url": "https://fungames.com"
    }
  ]
}

That's it! Upptime will start checking these websites right away. It's like making a list for your robot friend!
