What is CentOS?

CentOS is like the manager of a computer - it helps everything run smoothly!

Keeps Things Organized

CentOS helps your computer keep track of all its files, pictures, and games - like a super neat organizer!

Keeps It Safe

It helps protect your computer from bad stuff, like a security guard watching over everything!

Makes It Fast

CentOS helps your computer work quickly, so your games and programs run smoothly!

How Does CentOS Work?

It's like having a helpful manager for your computer!

1 Starts Your Computer

When you turn on your computer, CentOS wakes everything up and gets ready for you to use it - like a morning alarm!

2 Manages Programs

It helps open and close your games and programs, making sure they have space to run - like a traffic controller!

3 Stores Your Stuff

CentOS keeps track of all your pictures, videos, and school projects in neat folders - like a digital backpack!

4 Updates Regularly

It gets better over time with updates that add new features and stay safe - like learning new tricks!
