About Doubao AI

Doubao AI is an advanced artificial intelligence assistant developed to provide users with intelligent responses and assistance across various domains. From programming help to content creation, Doubao is designed to understand context and deliver relevant, helpful information.

The project began in 2022 with a team of AI researchers and engineers passionate about making artificial intelligence more accessible and useful for everyday tasks. What started as a side project quickly evolved into a full-fledged AI assistant with a growing community of users.

Technology Stack

Machine Learning

Built on transformer architectures with custom fine-tuning for specific tasks. Utilizes PyTorch for model training and inference.

Backend

Python with FastAPI for the REST API, Redis for caching, and PostgreSQL for data storage.

Frontend

React.js with TypeScript for the web interface, styled with Tailwind CSS.
