What is Runway?

Runway is a leading AI-powered creative platform that makes machine learning accessible to artists, designers, and creators of all kinds.

Revolutionizing Creative Workflow

Founded in 2018, Runway has quickly become an essential tool for creative professionals, offering a suite of AI models and tools that integrate seamlessly into existing workflows.

The platform allows users to generate images, edit videos, remove backgrounds, and much more—all powered by state-of-the-art machine learning models, without requiring expertise in AI or programming.

The Meaning Behind the Name

"Runway" symbolizes the launchpad for creative ideas. Just as a runway enables airplanes to take flight, Runway provides creators with the tools and space to launch their creative visions into reality, powered by AI technology.
