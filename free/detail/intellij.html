What is IntelliJ Anyway?

It's a special tool that helps people make all sorts of fun things on the computer!

Make Games

Create your own video games with colorful characters and cool levels. Imagine making a game where a dragon collects coins!

Build Robots

Write code that tells robots what to do. You could make a robot dance, draw pictures, or even clean your room (maybe!)

Design Art

Use code to make beautiful pictures and animations. It's like painting with words instead of a brush!

How Do You Use It?

It's easier than you think! Just follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open It Up

Click on the colorful IntelliJ icon to start. It looks like a little brain with ideas!

1
2

Step 2: Start Something New

Click "New Project" to begin. It's like getting a fresh piece of paper to draw on!

Step 3: Type Your Code

Write special words that tell the computer what to do. It's like giving the computer a treasure map!

3
4

Step 4: See What Happens!

Click the "Run" button to watch your creation come to life. It's like pressing play on your favorite movie!
