What is OBS Studio?

OBS Studio is a super cool free tool that lets you create awesome videos and live streams!

Think of it like having your own TV studio on your computer. You can show games you're playing, draw pictures, or even make your own shows!

How to Use OBS Studio?

  1. 1

    Download & Install

    Get OBS Studio from the official website and install it on your computer.

  2. 2

    Add Sources

    Click the "+" button to add what you want to show (game, camera, screen).

  3. 3

    Arrange Things

    Move and resize your sources to look just how you want them.

  4. 4

    Start Creating!

    Click "Start Recording" to save your video or "Start Streaming" to share live.

Try This Simple Code!

// This is like magic words for OBS
function startCreating() {
  addSource("game");
  addSource("camera");
  arrangeSourcesNicely();
  pressButton("record");
  haveFun();
}
// Let's do it!
startCreating();
