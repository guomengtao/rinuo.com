What Are W3Schools Animations?

What It Is

W3Schools animations are fun, colorful visual tools that help kids understand basic web concepts through play and exploration.

Purpose

These animations make learning about web technologies easy and enjoyable, building a foundation for future digital skills.

How to Use

Click on any animation, watch how things work, and interact with elements to see how web pages come to life!
