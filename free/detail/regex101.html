How Does It Work?

It's easy! Let's look at some simple examples.

Finding Letters

This pattern finds all the letter "a"s in a sentence:

/a/g

Text:

"Apples are red and tasty"

Finds:

"a", "a", "a"

Finding Words

This pattern finds all words that start with "c":

/\bc\w+/g

Text:

"Cats like to chase cars and climb trees"

Finds:

"Cats", "chase", "cars", "climb"
