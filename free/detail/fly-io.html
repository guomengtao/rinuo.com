What is Fly.io?

Imagine you made a cool game or a fun website on your computer. Fly.io helps share it with kids all over the world!

World Traveler

Fly.io takes your code and puts it on computers all around the world, so everyone can play with it quickly!

Super Fast

It makes sure your creations load super fast, no matter if someone is in New York, London, or Tokyo!

Safety Guard

Fly.io watches over your code, keeping it safe and making sure it runs smoothly 24/7!
