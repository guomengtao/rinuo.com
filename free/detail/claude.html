What is Claude?

A Friendly Helper

Claude is an AI that loves to help with homework, answer questions, and even help you create stories or drawings!

Super Smart

He knows a lot about science, math, history, and more! Just ask him anything you're curious about.

Easy to Talk To

You can chat with Claude just like you would with a friend. He speaks your language!

Safe & Kind

Claude is always polite and makes sure to keep conversations friendly and appropriate.

How to Use Claude

Start a Conversation

Just type a message to Claude, like you would in a chat app. Say hello or ask a question!

1

You: Hi Claude! Can you help me with my math homework?

Claude: Hi there! Of course I can help with math. What problem are you working on?

2

Get a Response

Claude will reply to your message with helpful information or ask for more details.

Keep Chatting

You can keep asking more questions or ask Claude to explain things in a different way.

3

You: Can you explain how to solve 2 + 2 × 3? I'm confused.

Fun Things to Do with Claude

Here are some cool ways kids use Claude every day!

Homework Help

Claude can explain tricky math problems or help with science questions.

Try: "Can you show me how to divide 56 by 7?"

Story Time

Ask Claude to create fun stories about dragons, space adventures, or anything you like!

Try: "Tell me a story about a penguin who wants to fly."

Art Ideas

Get creative suggestions for drawings, crafts, and other art projects.

Try: "What's a fun drawing I can make with only circles?"

Learn New Things

Games & Puzzles
