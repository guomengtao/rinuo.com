Project Origins

Founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers, Anthropic aims to develop safe and beneficial AI systems.

Claude emerged from the team's research into constitutional AI - a method to align AI systems with human values through principles rather than specific behaviors.

The first version was released in 2022, with continuous improvements based on user feedback and safety research.

Strengths & Limitations

An honest assessment of Claude's capabilities and areas for improvement.

Strengths

  • Superior safety compared to many alternatives

  • Excellent at following complex instructions

  • Strong performance with long documents

  • Transparent about its limitations

  • Ability to revise its own outputs

  • Good at maintaining context in long conversations

Limitations

  • Limited knowledge of events after July 2024

  • Occasionally overly cautious in responses

  • No built-in internet access for real-time info

  • Limited creative capabilities compared to some alternatives

  • Free tier has usage limitations

  • Less powerful for code generation tasks
