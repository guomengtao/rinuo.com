What is Excalidraw?

Imagine having a super easy drawing tool that works right in your web browser. That's Excalidraw!

It's like having a digital sketchbook where you can draw anything you want - from simple shapes to amazing stories, cool science diagrams, or even your favorite video game characters!

Why Use Excalidraw?

  • Super easy to use - no complicated buttons!
  • Looks like hand-drawn sketches - so cool!
  • Works on any computer, tablet, or even phone
  • You can share your drawings with friends easily
  • It's completely free to use!
