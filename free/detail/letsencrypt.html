What is Let's Encrypt?

Let's Encrypt is like having a super strong, invisible shield for websites! It protects the information you send and receive when you're online.

Imagine it's like putting a secret code on your messages so only the right people can read them - just like passing notes in class without others seeing!

Keeps You Safe

Let's Encrypt makes sure bad guys can't sneak a peek at what you do online, like your drawings or messages.

Secret Codes

It uses special secret codes to lock your information, like a treasure chest with a magic key!

Everywhere Free

Let's Encrypt gives this protection to every website for free, so all kids can be safe online!

Super Fast

It works so quickly you won't even notice it's there, keeping you safe without slowing down your games!

How to Spot a Safe Website

It's easy to tell if Let's Encrypt is protecting a website. Look for these clues:

https:// funwebsite.com
Secure!

Look for the "S" in HTTPS

Safe websites start with https:// - the "s" stands for "secure"!

You'll also see a little lock icon next to the website name. That means Let's Encrypt is working hard to protect you!

Safe Signs

  • Lock icon in the address bar
  • Website address starts with https://
  • No scary warning messages

Warning Signs

  • No lock icon in the address bar
  • Website address starts with http:// (no "s")
  • Your browser shows a warning message
