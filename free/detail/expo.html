Let's Explore

What Can You Do With Expo?

Play Learning Games

Enjoy fun games that help with counting, letters, shapes, and so much more! Learn while having a blast.

Create Amazing Art

Unleash your creativity with colorful drawing tools and fun stickers. Make masterpieces to share!

Sing & Dance Along

Move to catchy tunes and learn new songs that teach about animals, numbers, and the world around us.

Listen to Stories

Explore exciting stories with colorful pictures and friendly voices that bring characters to life.

How to Use Expo

1

Download the App

Get Expo from the App Store or Google Play. It's free and easy to install on any device!

2

Create Your Profile

Set up a fun profile with a cool avatar. Parents can help with this quick and simple step.

3

Start Exploring

Choose from games, stories, or activities. Follow the simple instructions and have fun learning!

4

Track Your Progress

See all the cool things you've learned and accomplished. Unlock badges for new skills!
