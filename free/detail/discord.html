What is Discord?

Discord is a fun place to chat, share, and hang out with your friends online!

What is Discord, Anyway?

Imagine a treehouse where you and your friends can talk, share pictures, and play games together - but online!

That's what Discord is like! It's a safe place to hang out with people you know, even when you can't be together in person.

Talk in Many Ways

You can type messages, send pictures, or even talk out loud with your voice - like a phone call with lots of friends!

Different Rooms for Different Things

There are special spaces for talking about games, homework, or sharing your favorite drawings - just like different corners in a treehouse!

What Can You Do on Discord?

Chat with Friends

Send messages, emojis, and pictures to your friends. It's like passing notes but way faster!

Play Games Together

Talk while playing your favorite games. You can plan strategies or just laugh when something funny happens!

Share Your Creations

Show off your drawings, crafts, or cool projects. Your friends can give you compliments and ideas!

Staying Safe on Discord

Keep Personal Info Private

Don't share your address, phone number, school name, or other private information with people you don't know well.

Only Talk to People You Know

It's best to use Discord with friends from school, sports, or other activities you do in real life.

Tell a Grown-Up

If someone says something mean or makes you feel uncomfortable, tell a parent, teacher, or another grown-up you trust.

Use Privacy Settings

Ask a grown-up to help set up your privacy settings so only people you know can message you.
