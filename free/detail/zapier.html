What Can Zapier Do?

Email to Chat

Zapier can take important emails and automatically send them to your chat app, so you never miss anything!

Like: "When I get a special email, tell my team on Slack!"

Share Across Apps

Zapier can take your photos from one app and share them to another app automatically!

How Do You Use Zapier?

1

Pick Your Apps

Choose which two apps you want to connect. It can be any apps you use, like games, drawing tools, or learning apps!

Instagram Google Docs TikTok And 5000+ more!
2

Make a Zap

A "Zap" is what we call the magic connection between apps. It's like creating a little robot helper!

Example Zap:

"When I take a photo (Trigger), save it to my folder (Action)"

3

Turn It On

Once you've set up your Zap, just turn it on! Now your apps will work together automatically, even when you're not using them.

Zap is ON and working for you!
