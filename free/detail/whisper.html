What Exactly is Whisper?

Whisper is a special computer program created by smart people at OpenAI. It can listen to what you say (your voice) and turn it into written words!

It's like magic dictation - you talk, and Whisper writes it down perfectly. It can even understand different languages and people with different accents!

How It Helps

Whisper can help you write stories just by talking, or take notes when you're in a hurry!

Many Languages

It understands over 99 different languages! From English to Spanish to Chinese and more.

Super Smart

It can even figure out what you meant to say, even if you make a mistake or pause!

Private & Safe

Whisper keeps your words private and safe, just for you to see.

How Does Whisper Work?

It's easier than you think! Let's break it down in 4 simple steps:

You Speak

You talk into a microphone. It could be your phone, tablet, or computer. Say anything you want!

1
2

Whisper Listens

Whisper listens to your voice and turns the sounds into special computer code that it can understand.

Whisper Thinks

Whisper uses its super brain to figure out exactly what words you're saying, even if there's a little noise around you.

3
4

Words Appear!

Finally, Whisper shows you the words it heard! You can read them, change them, or save them for later.
