What is Postwoman?

Imagine you have two friends who want to talk to each other, but they live in different houses. How do they send messages? They could use a mail carrier!

Postwoman is like a super fast mail carrier, but for computers! She helps one computer send messages to another computer, even if they're far apart on the internet.

Sends Messages

Carries information between different computers

Super Fast

Delivers messages in just a blink of an eye

Reliable

Makes sure messages arrive safely

How Does Postwoman Work?

1

Write a Message

First, a computer writes a message it wants to send. It could be a question, some information, or a request for something.

{"message": "Can I have the weather report?"}
2

Postwoman Picks It Up

Postwoman takes the message and finds the best way to deliver it to the right computer.

> Postwoman received your message!
> Finding the destination computer...
> Route found! Sending now...
3

Message Delivered

The other computer gets the message, writes a reply, and Postwoman brings it back!

{"reply": "Today will be sunny and warm!"}
4

Conversation Happens

This process can happen back and forth, creating a conversation between computers!

Can you tell me a joke?

Why did the computer go to the doctor? Because it had a virus!
