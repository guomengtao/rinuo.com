What is CircleCL?

CircleCL is your super tool for creating cool projects with code! It's like building with digital blocks, but you get to make games, art, and so much more.

Easy to Learn

CircleCL uses simple commands that are easy to remember. You'll be creating your first project in minutes!

Be Creative

Make colorful drawings, fun animations, and even simple games. The only limit is your imagination!

Share Your Work

Show off your creations to friends and family. See what others are making too!

How Does It Work?

Using CircleCL is as easy as 1, 2, 3! Let's see how you can start creating.

1

Write Simple Commands

Use easy words like "draw", "move", and "color" to tell CircleCL what to do. It's like giving directions to a friend!

2

See Instant Results

Watch your creation come to life right away! If you want to change something, just edit your commands and see the new result.

3

Save & Share

When you're happy with your project, save it and share it with others. You can even keep improving it later!

circlecl_demo.cl
start_circle(100)
color("blue")
fill()
move(200, 0)
color("red")
start_circle(75)
fill()
// This makes two circles!
