The Story Behind DeepSeek

DeepSeek was founded in 2022 by a team of AI researchers and engineers with backgrounds from leading tech companies and academic institutions. Frustrated by the complexity and inaccessibility of state-of-the-art AI models, they set out to create a platform that would make advanced AI capabilities available to everyone.

What started as a research project quickly evolved into a full-fledged platform, with the team releasing their first open-source model in early 2023. Since then, DeepSeek has grown exponentially, attracting contributors from around the world and building a vibrant community of AI enthusiasts.
