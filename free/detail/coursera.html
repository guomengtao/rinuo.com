What is Coursera?

Coursera is like an online school with thousands of cool classes! But instead of going to a building, you can learn from your computer, tablet, or even phone.

The best part? You can learn about almost anything - from space and dinosaurs to making art or building robots!

Top universities from around the world teach on Coursera!

How Coursera Works in 3 Easy Steps

1

Find a Course You Love

Browse through thousands of courses about science, art, music, technology, and more until you find something that excites you!

2

Learn at Your Own Pace

Watch videos, do fun activities, and take quizzes whenever you want - in your room, on the bus, or even on vacation!

3

Earn Certificates

When you finish a course, you get a special certificate to show everyone what you've learned!

How Do You Use Coursera?

  1. 1

    Create an Account

    Go to Coursera.org and sign up for a free account. You'll need a parent's help to do this part!

  2. 2

    Explore Courses

    Use the search bar to find topics you're interested in. You can filter to find free courses too!

  3. 3

    Enroll in a Course

    When you find something cool, click "Enroll" to add it to your list of courses. It's that easy!

  4. 4

    Start Learning!

    Watch the video lessons, complete the activities, and track your progress as you learn new things!
