What is AWS anyway?

It's like a magical playground for computers, but in the sky!

Cloud Computers

AWS has special computers that live in big buildings. They help your games and videos work super fast!

Games & Apps

Many of your favorite games use AWS to make sure you can play with friends, even if they're far away!

Videos & Fun

AWS helps shows and cartoons stream smoothly on your tablet or TV without stopping!

See AWS in Action!

Look at this simple code that uses AWS magic!

aws-terminal-for-kids
Welcome to AWS Kids Mode!
