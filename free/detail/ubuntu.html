What is Ubuntu?

Ubuntu is like the magical foundation of a computer! It's what makes your computer work and lets you play games, learn, and create awesome things.

Just like how a playground has rules to keep everyone safe and happy, Ubuntu helps computers work smoothly and safely for kids like you!

It's Safe & Friendly

Ubuntu keeps bad stuff away from your computer, so you can explore without worries!

Super Fast

Ubuntu makes computers run quickly, so your games and learning programs work better!

Fun to Customize

You can change how Ubuntu looks - like giving your computer a new outfit!

Totally Free!

Ubuntu doesn't cost any money - it's a special gift for everyone to use!

What Problems Does Ubuntu Solve?

Stops Bad Bugs

Ubuntu keeps mean computer bugs (called viruses) away that try to hurt your computer.

Fixes Slow Computers

Ubuntu makes old computers run fast again, so you don't need a new one to have fun.

Keeps You Safe

Ubuntu helps protect your information and keeps bad people from seeing what you do online.
