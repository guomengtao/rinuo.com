Let's Learn! Try It Now

What is Zoom Anyway?

Zoom is like a magical window that lets you see and talk to people who aren't in the same room as you!

Virtual Meetings

Talk to your friends, classmates, or family when you can't meet in person. It's like having a party on your computer!

Online Classes

Join your lessons from home! See your teacher, ask questions, and learn new things with your classmates.

Fun Activities

Play games, have show-and-tell, or even celebrate birthdays with friends who live far away!

How Does Zoom Work?

It's easy! Just follow these simple steps to start using Zoom.

1. Get the Zoom App

Ask a grown-up to help you download Zoom on a computer, tablet, or phone. It's free!

1
2

2. Join a Meeting

When it's time to meet, enter the special meeting code (like a secret password) that your teacher or friend gives you.

3. Turn On Your Camera & Mic

Let everyone see and hear you! Look for the camera and microphone buttons to turn them on.

3
4

4. Have Fun!

Talk, laugh, learn, and enjoy your virtual gathering! When you're done, just click "Leave Meeting".
