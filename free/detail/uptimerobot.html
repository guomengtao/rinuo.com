What is UptimeRobot?

UptimeRobot is a powerful yet user-friendly website monitoring service that checks your websites, servers, and APIs at regular intervals. It alerts you immediately when your service goes down via email, SMS, Slack, or other integrations, ensuring you can resolve issues before your users notice.

Reliable Monitoring

Checks your websites every 5 minutes (free plan) or as frequently as every 30 seconds (premium plans) from multiple global locations.

Instant Alerts

Receive notifications through various channels the moment your service goes down or experiences issues.

Detailed Reports

Access comprehensive uptime reports, performance metrics, and historical data to analyze your service's reliability.

Key Features

UptimeRobot offers a comprehensive set of features to monitor your online services effectively

Multi-type Monitoring

Monitor HTTP(s), Ping, Port, DNS, Keyword, and more. Supports custom HTTP headers, authentication, and SSL expiration alerts.

Global Check Locations

Checks from multiple global locations to ensure accurate uptime monitoring regardless of geographical location.

Performance Tracking

Monitor response times and receive alerts when your website performance drops below acceptable levels.

API Access

Integrate monitoring data into your own applications using the comprehensive REST API.

Team Collaboration

Add team members with different permission levels to share monitoring responsibilities.

Third-party Integrations

Connect with services like Slack, Telegram, Microsoft Teams, Zapier, and more for notifications and workflow automation.

About UptimeRobot

Origin & Name Meaning

UptimeRobot was founded in 2012 by Berk Demir with a simple mission: to provide an accessible, reliable website monitoring service for everyone.

The name "UptimeRobot" reflects the service's core function – a robotic system that continuously checks and ensures your website's uptime, working tirelessly 24/7 like a robot.

Founder

Berk Demir

A software developer with a passion for creating accessible tools for web professionals. Berk started UptimeRobot to address the lack of affordable, user-friendly monitoring solutions.

Latest News

In 2025, UptimeRobot launched enhanced AI-powered anomaly detection and improved mobile apps for on-the-go monitoring. The service now supports over 2 million users worldwide.
