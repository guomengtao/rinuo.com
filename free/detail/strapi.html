Meet Strapi!

Your digital treasure chest organizer!

What is Strapi?

Imagine you have a huge toy box full of all your favorite things. Strapi is like a super organized toy box for digital stuff - but way cooler!

Strapi helps you:

  • Keep track of photos, videos, and stories in one place
  • Organize your digital projects neatly
  • Share your creations with friends easily
  • Change and update your stuff whenever you want
Strapi

How Does Strapi Work?

1

Create Your Collection

Tell Strapi what kind of things you want to store - like photos from your vacation or your favorite recipes!

2

Add Your Stuff

Put your digital things into Strapi. It's like putting toys on special shelves in your toy box.

3

Use and Share!

Now you can easily find, show, or share your things whenever you want. Strapi keeps everything organized!

See Strapi in Action

Imagine using Strapi to organize your favorite books. Let's see how that would work!

// Let's make a book collection with Strapi!
// 1. First, we create a "Book" collection
const bookCollection = {
  name: "Book",
  fields: [
    { name: "title", type: "text" },
    { name: "author", type: "text" },
    { name: "pages", type: "number" },
    { name: "favorite", type: "yes/no" }
  ]
};

// 2. Then we add our books
const myBooks = [
  {
    title: "Space Adventure",
    author: "Jane Smith",
    pages: 120,
    favorite: "yes"
  },
  {
    title: "Ocean Mysteries",
    author: "Bob Johnson",
    pages: 95,
    favorite: "yes"
  }
];
