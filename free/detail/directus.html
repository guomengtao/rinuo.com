What is Directus?

Directus is like a magical toolbox for information! It helps you collect, organize, and use all kinds of cool data.

Stores Your Stuff

Keep all your favorite things safe - like game scores, drawings, or even your pet's funny habits!

Looks Awesome

Make your information look super cool with colors, pictures, and fun layouts that you can change anytime!

Share With Friends

Show your projects to friends and family, or even work together on something amazing!

How Does It Work?

It's easy! You can use simple commands to tell Directus what to do. Let's try some examples.

Creating a new game score list
// Let's make a place to keep track of our game scores
const gameScores = new DirectusCollection('My Awesome Games');

// Add some games to our list
gameScores.add({
name: 'Space Adventure',
score: 1500,
date: 'today'
});

// Show our scores to friends
gameScores.share('with my best friend');
