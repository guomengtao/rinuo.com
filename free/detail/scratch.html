Let's Get Started

What is Scratch?

Scratch is a free programming language and online community where you can create your own interactive stories, games, and animations.

Block-Based Coding

Instead of typing code, you snap together colorful blocks. It's like building with digital Legos!

Create Anything

Make your own games, animated stories, interactive art, and much more. The only limit is your imagination!

Share with Others

Publish your projects online and share them with millions of creators around the world.

How Does It Work?

It's easy to start creating with Scratch's colorful code blocks. Just drag, drop, and snap them together!

Simple Steps to Create

1

Choose Your Sprites

Pick characters and objects for your project. You can use the ones provided or draw your own!

2

Snap Together Blocks

Use colorful code blocks to tell your sprites what to do. No typing required!

3

Test Your Project

Click the green flag to run your project. Make changes if something doesn't work.

4

Share with Others

Publish your creation so friends and kids around the world can see and play with it!
