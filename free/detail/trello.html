Let's Get Started!

What is Trello?

Trello is like a digital bulletin board where you can organize anything you want!

Boards

Think of a Board as your main project. It could be for your science fair, a video game plan, or your summer vacation!

Lists

Lists help you sort your ideas. You could have "To Do", "Doing", and "Done" lists for your homework!

Cards

Cards are like sticky notes. Write tasks, ideas, or notes on them and move them between lists!

How to Use Trello

It's super easy! Just follow these simple steps:

1

Create a Board

Click the "+" button and select "Create Board". Give it a cool name like "My Awesome Science Project"!

Click "Create new board" → Name it → Click "Create"
2

Add Lists

Create lists to organize your project. Try "Ideas", "Planning", and "Finished" for starters!

Click "Add a list" → Name it → Click "Add List"
3

Make Cards & Move Them

Add cards to your lists and drag them around as you make progress. It's like magic!

Click "Add a card" → Type your idea → Drag and drop!
