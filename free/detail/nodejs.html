What is Node.js?

Node.js is like a magical playground where computers can follow instructions and play games!

It helps computers:

  • Talk to other computers around the world
  • Play music and show pictures
  • Remember your favorite games
  • Do math problems super fast

How Does It Work?

1

You Write a Note

You create simple instructions using special words that computers understand.

2

Computer Reads It

Node.js helps the computer read your note and understand what to do.

3

Magic Happens!

The computer follows your instructions and creates something amazing!
