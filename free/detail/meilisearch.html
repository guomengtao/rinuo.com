About Meilisearch

A powerful, open-source search engine designed to be simple to use and integrate into any project.

Project Origin

Meilisearch was born in 2018 out of frustration with existing search solutions that were either too complex, too slow, or too expensive. The founding team wanted to create a search engine that could deliver instant, relevant results while being accessible to developers of all skill levels.

What started as a side project quickly gained traction in the developer community, evolving into a robust solution used by thousands of projects worldwide.

What Makes Meilisearch Different

A balanced look at the strengths and limitations of Meilisearch

Advantages

  • Blazing fast search responses (usually under 50ms)
  • Excellent out-of-the-box relevance with minimal configuration
  • Simple integration with RESTful API and SDKs for major languages
  • Lightweight and easy to deploy on any infrastructure
  • Rich feature set: typo tolerance, filtering, sorting, and more
  • Active development and responsive community support

Limitations

  • Not ideal for extremely large datasets (billions of documents)
  • Limited advanced analytics compared to enterprise solutions
  • Some advanced features require manual configuration
  • Smaller ecosystem than established search engines
  • Full-text search focused, less optimized for vector search
  • Limited multi-language support compared to specialized solutions
