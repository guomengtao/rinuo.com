The Story Behind Cursor

Cursor began in 2022 as a side project when James and Sarah were frustrated with how disconnected AI coding tools felt from their editor workflow.

They wanted an editor where AI felt like a natural extension of the coding process, not a separate tool you had to switch to. What started as a weekend experiment quickly gained traction among their developer friends.

After raising a $10M seed round from top VC firms in early 2023, the team expanded and released the first public beta. Within six months, Cursor had over 100,000 active users and continues to grow rapidly.
