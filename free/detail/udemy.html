What is Udemy?

Udemy is like a huge playground for learning! It's a website where you can find all kinds of fun courses to help you learn new skills.

Imagine having a magic book that can teach you how to draw cartoons, make cool games, speak new languages, or even become a science detective! That's what Udemy is, but even better because you can watch videos and practice as you learn.

Thousands of teachers from around the world have created special courses just for kids like you. And the best part? You can learn at your own pace, anytime you want!
