What is Commitizen?

Imagine you have a special notebook where you write down all your cool ideas and drawings.

Sometimes, when you add new things to your notebook, it gets messy. You might forget what you wrote!

Commitizen is like a friendly helper that helps you keep your notebook neat and organized. It makes sure you always remember what you added!

How Does It Work?

1

You Create Something

Draw a picture, write a story, or build something cool!

2

Tell Commitizen

Explain what you made, so you'll remember later!

3

Keep It Organized

Commitizen helps keep everything neat and easy to find!
