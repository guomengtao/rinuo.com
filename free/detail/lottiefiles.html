What are Lottie Animations?

Imagine you draw a picture of a cat. Now, what if that cat could move - walk, jump, and meow?

That's what LottieFiles do! They are special files that make drawings come to life on phones, tablets, and computers.

They're like tiny movies

But much smaller! They don't take up much space, so they load super fast.

They work everywhere

On phones, tablets, computers - even smart watches! They look perfect everywhere.

What Can LottieFiles Do?

Make Things Happy

Animations can smile, dance, and celebrate when you do something great in a game or app!

Show You How

They can teach you things by showing steps in a fun way, like how to tie shoes or solve a puzzle!

Play Games

Animations make games more fun! Characters can run, jump, and do cool tricks because of LottieFiles.

How Do You Use LottieFiles?

let happyAnimation = new Lottie('smile.json');
happyAnimation.play();
happyAnimation.loop = true;
happyAnimation.speed = 2; // Make it faster!

Grown-ups who make apps and games use simple code like this to add animations!

They can make animations go fast or slow, big or small, and even change colors!
