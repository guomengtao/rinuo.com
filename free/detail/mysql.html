What is MySQL?

MySQL is like a magical filing cabinet for storing information! Instead of papers, it stores data in special tables.

Think of it as a super organized toy box where you can easily find exactly what you're looking for, like your favorite games or toys.

Websites, apps, and games use MySQL to keep track of all their information, from scores in games to your favorite videos!
