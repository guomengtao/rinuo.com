Let's Get Started

What is ScratchJr?

ScratchJr is an awesome app that lets you create your own interactive stories, games, and animations!

Instead of typing complicated code, you snap together colorful blocks - like puzzle pieces! Each block does something different, like making a character move, jump, or change color.

You can make characters talk, create your own games, or tell fun stories. The best part? You can share what you make with friends and family!

"With ScratchJr, you're not just playing games - you're making them!"

How Does It Work?

It's easy to start creating with these simple steps!

1

Choose Characters

Pick from cool characters or draw your own! You can have cats, dinosaurs, robots, or anything you imagine.

2

Snap Blocks

Click and drag colorful blocks to make things happen. Want your character to move? Use a move block!

3

Play & Share

Press the green flag to watch your creation come to life! Then show it to your friends and family.
