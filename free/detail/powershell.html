Try These Simple Commands

Copy and paste these into PowerShell to see what happens!

Say Hello

Make your computer say hello to you!

Write-Host "Hello, I'm your computer!"

What it does: Displays a friendly message on your screen.

Show Your Name

Tell the computer your name and have it remember it!

$myName = "Your Name"
Write-Host "Hello, $myName!"

What it does: Stores your name and then says hello using it.

Count to 10

Watch the computer count from 1 to 10!

1..10 | ForEach-Object { Write-Host $_ }

What it does: Displays numbers 1 through 10, each on a new line.
