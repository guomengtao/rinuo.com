What is Linux Anyway?

Let's keep it simple! Linux is like the boss of a computer. It tells the computer what to do.

Computer Boss

Linux is the boss that helps your computer understand what you want it to do, like playing games or drawing pictures.

Totally Free

The cool thing? Linux is free! Anyone can use it, share it, and even change it to make it better.

Everywhere!

Linux isn't just on computers. It's in phones, TVs, game consoles, and even some toys!
