What is Codemoji?

Coding made simple and fun!

Codemoji is a cool way to learn programming. Instead of complicated words, we use emojis to write code. It's like learning a secret language that computers understand, but with pictures you already know!

Whether you want to make games, animations, or your own websites, Codemoji helps you learn the basics while having a blast. No experience needed - just bring your curiosity!
