What is Gmail?

Send Messages

Write notes to people and send them instantly through the computer! It's like magic mail that arrives in seconds.

Share Pictures

Show your drawings or photos to friends and family by adding them to your messages. They'll see them right away!

Get Replies

When you send a message, people can write back to you! You'll get their reply in your own special mailbox.

How to Use Gmail

1 Open Gmail

Go to gmail.com on your computer or open the Gmail app on a tablet or phone.

Browser
Type: gmail.com
Press: Enter

2 Click "Compose"

Find the big red button that says "Compose" and click it. This opens a new message!

3 Write Your Message

Fill in who you're sending to, add a subject, and write your message. You can even add emojis!

4 Send It!

Click the send button (it looks like a paper airplane) and your message will fly to its destination!
