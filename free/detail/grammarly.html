Meet Grammarly!

Your super helper for better writing!

Let's Explore

What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is like having a super smart friend who helps you write better! It checks your writing and finds mistakes in:

Spelling

Like when you write "teh" instead of "the"

Grammar

Like knowing when to use "is" or "are"

Punctuation

Helping you know where to put . ! or ?

Words

Suggesting better words to use

It's like magic for your writing! ✨

What Does Grammarly Do?

Catches Mistakes

Grammarly finds errors in your writing and shows you how to fix them.

Gives Ideas

It suggests better words to make your writing sound awesome!

Makes Writing Fun

When your writing is clear and correct, it's more fun to read!

See It In Action!

This is how Grammarly helps fix writing:

Before (with mistakes):

I goed to the park with my friendes we play happily.

After (fixed by Grammarly):

I went to the park with my friends. We played happily.

How Do You Use Grammarly?

Step 1: Get Grammarly

Ask a grown-up to help you get Grammarly on the computer or tablet. It's easy to add!

1
2

Step 2: Start Writing

Write your story, homework, or message like you normally do. Grammarly will watch as you write!

Step 3: Look for Suggestions

Grammarly will show you little squiggly lines where there might be mistakes. Click on them to see fixes!

3
4

Step 4: Make It Better

Choose the best suggestion to fix your writing. Your words will look awesome!
