Let's Try Some R Magic!

R Console
> my_numbers <- c(1, 2, 3, 4, 5)
> my_numbers
[1] 1 2 3 4 5
> sum(my_numbers)
[1] 15
> mean(my_numbers)
[1] 3

What's happening here?

  1. 1 We made a list of numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
  2. 2 We asked R to show us the numbers
  3. 3 We asked R to add them all up (sum) - the answer is 15!
  4. 4 We asked R for the average (mean) - the answer is 3!
