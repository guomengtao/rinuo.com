What is Unity?

Unity is a special computer program that helps you create your own games, animations, and interactive experiences!

Many of the games you love to play were made with Unity. It's used by professional game creators all over the world, but it's also perfect for beginners!

Make Games

Create 2D and 3D games for computers, phones, and even VR headsets!

Animate

Bring characters and objects to life with cool animations.

Share Easily

Show your creations to friends and family around the world.

How Does Unity Work?

Use Simple Code

Unity uses a special language called C#. Don't worry, it's like giving simple instructions to your computer! 

// Make a ball jump when you press space
if (Input.GetKeyDown(KeyCode.Space)) {
    GetComponent<Rigidbody>().AddForce(Vector3.up * jumpForce);
}

Drag and Drop

You can create many things in Unity just by dragging and dropping. It's like building with digital blocks!

  • Add characters to your game
  • Create environments like forests or cities
  • Add sounds and special effects
  • Make objects move and interact
