So, What Exactly Is Hacker News?

Hacker News is like a giant bulletin board on the internet where people post all sorts of interesting things about technology!

Imagine a place where you can find stories about new inventions, cool apps, amazing games, and even learn how to make your own!

People share links to articles, videos, and projects they think are awesome, and then everyone can talk about them in the comments.

Fun Facts

  • Started in 2007 by the same people who made Reddit!
  • Millions of people visit every month
  • Simple design so you focus on the stories

How Does It Work?

  1. 1

    People share links

    Someone finds a cool article or project and shares it on Hacker News

  2. 2

    Voting happens

    Others can click the up arrow if they like the story - this moves it higher on the page!

  3. 3

    Discussions start

    People can leave comments to talk about the story, ask questions, or share their ideas
