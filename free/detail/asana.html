Let's Play with Asana!

Fun movements for happy kids!

Move your body, have fun!

What's Asana?

Asana is just a fancy word for "poses" or "positions" you can put your body in!

It's like playing a game of "freeze" where you hold funny and interesting shapes.

Doing these poses helps you stretch, balance, and feel strong - all while having fun!

Try These Fun Poses!

Can you make your body look like these things? Let's try!

Tree Pose

Stand tall like a tree! Put one foot on your other leg and stretch your arms up like branches.

Look at something steady to help you balance!

Frog Pose

Squat down low like a frog! Spread your knees wide and put your hands on the floor.

Can you jump like a frog from this position?

Butterfly Pose

Sit down and bring your feet together like butterfly wings! Flap your legs gently.

Make fluttering butterfly sounds while you move!

Asana Challenge Quiz!

Can you guess which animal or thing these asanas represent?

1. In this pose, you lie on your stomach and lift your arms and legs off the ground. What is it called?

2. This pose has you on your hands and knees, arching your back like an angry animal. What is it?

3. In this pose, you stand with legs wide apart and reach one hand down to touch your ankle. What is it?
