How to Claim Free Resources

Most free resources offer free plans for individual developers, students, and small teams. Here's how to get started

Create Account

Visit the official website and register an account. Most platforms support quick registration with GitHub or Google.

Select Free Plan

After registration, select the free plan from the account settings or pricing page. Some resources may require identity verification.

Complete Verification

Some resources (like student plans) require proof documentation. Once verified, you can start using the free resources.

Important Notes

  • Free plans usually have resource limits like storage capacity and request quotas
  • Commercial use may require upgrading to a paid plan, please read the terms of service carefully
  • Students and educational institutions can often get additional free resources, check for education plans
  • Most platforms provide detailed documentation and tutorials to help you get started quickly
