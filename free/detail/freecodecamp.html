What is freeCodeCamp?

freeCodeCamp is a super cool website where you can learn how to code for FREE! It's like a fun playground for learning computer skills.

Even if you've never coded before, freeCodeCamp makes it easy with simple lessons, interactive challenges, and even games that teach you how to make your own websites and apps!

What Can You Learn?

Lots of cool stuff! Here are some of the fun things you can learn on freeCodeCamp:

HTML & CSS

Learn how to build your own websites! Make colorful pages with pictures, buttons, and fun text styles.

JavaScript

Make your websites do cool things! Learn to create games, animations, and interactive buttons.

Design Basics

Learn how to make your websites look awesome with colors, layouts, and cool design tricks.

Simple AI Projects

Explore the basics of artificial intelligence with fun, easy projects even kids can understand!

Phone Apps

Learn the basics of making simple apps for phones and tablets that your friends can use!

Data Games

Play with data! Learn how to collect and show information in fun ways like charts and graphs.
