How to Get Started

1. Choose Your Provider

Select an email service based on your needs (personal, business, marketing) and budget.

2. Sign Up for an Account

Visit the provider's website and create an account. For free tiers, no payment is required.

3. Configure SMTP (Optional)

Use the provider's documentation to set up SMTP for third-party application integration.

4. Start Using Your Email

Begin sending and receiving emails, using additional features based on your selected plan.
