Our Story

FreeDevTools was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make high-quality developer resources accessible to everyone, regardless of budget or experience level.

We started as a small project to collect and organize useful free tools for our own development work, and quickly realized there was a huge demand for a centralized platform that brings together the best free resources from across the web.

Today, we're proud to offer thousands of carefully curated free tools, frameworks, templates, and learning materials for developers of all skill levels.
